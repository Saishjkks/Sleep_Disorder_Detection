Sleep Disorder Detection

Understand how sleep impacts your health and take steps to improve it.

Why is Sleep Important?

  • Improve memory and concentration
  • Boost the immune system
  • Reduce stress and anxiety
  • Regulate body weight
  • Enhance productivity and mood

What Happens If You Don't Sleep Well?

  • Increased risk of heart disease and high blood pressure
  • Weakened immune system
  • Poor concentration and memory loss
  • Mood swings, stress, and anxiety
  • Increased risk of diabetes and weight gain
  • Slow reaction time, leading to accidents
